Do you have some free time to donate to a worthy cause?

Whatever your interest may be, we have lots of bunnies at the shelter and all sorts of opportunities for you! What you have to gain by volunteering: Learn all about Bunny basics during a volunteer shift(s). Information covers diet, feeding, grooming, illnesses, habitat and general care. Our shelter is clean and fully air-conditioned and we are very proud of our facility. It is a fun and collaborative environment. The buns would love to see you! Beware! lovin’ on the buns can become habit-forming! The minimum age to volunteer is 16. If you are 16 or 17 we require a parent to be present with you at all times. If you are interested in becoming a volunteer, please fill out our online volunteer application After you submit your application, our volunteer coordinator will contact you regarding the next steps in becoming a volunteer. Thank you for your interest in helping the bunnies @ House Rabbit Resource Network.
